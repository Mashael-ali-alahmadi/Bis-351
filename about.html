About GlowCare

GlowCare is dedicated to helping people build healthy skincare routines with products designed for every skin type. We believe skincare should be simple, effective, and accessible for everyone.

Our Story

GlowCare Skincare is a brand founded in Al Khobar in 2023 by a group of girls who noticed that many skincare brands do not truly understand different skin types.

We believe that everyone deserves products that suit their skin. Many people want to take care of their skin but struggle to identify their skin type or follow the right routine — and this is exactly what we want to change.

Our mission is to help customers feel confident with products that combine quality, comfort, and simplicity for every skin concern and daily routine.

Our Vision

We aim to become a unique and trusted name in the skincare market. GlowCare strives to make a real difference by offering simple, effective, and inclusive skincare solutions for everyone.

Our Target

We focus on people who feel unsure about their skin and are looking for products made especially for them. Our target includes all age groups who want personalized and easy skincare guidance.

Our Values

Eco-Friendly

We aim to become a green company by reducing waste and caring about the impact of our production on the environment.

Cruelty-Free

GlowCare is committed to being cruelty-free. We never test our products on animals at any stage of production.

Customer Care

We always put our customers first. Our goal is to provide products and guidance that truly meet their needs.
